Dr. Raghunath Mashelkar

Chancellor

Padma Vibhushan | Former Director General, CSIR, Government of India

Dr. Raghunath Mashelkar is known globally for his pioneering research in polymer science and engineering, exceptional and transformative leadership of major Indian R&D institutions, playing a key role in pioneering the intellectual property movement in India and being a dominant force in shaping India's science, technology and innovation policies and performance in the post-liberalised India.

He served as the Director General of Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR), with thirty-eight laboratories for over eleven years. He was also the President of Indian National Science Academy and Chairman of National Innovation Foundation.

Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering from the University of Bombay. Post-doctoral research at the University of Salford, UK. Holds honorary doctorates from over 40 universities worldwide.

Fellow of the Royal Society (FRS), London. Fellow of the Royal Academy of Engineering (FREng), UK. Fellow of all three Indian Science Academies.

Recipient of the Padma Vibhushan — India's second highest civilian honour. JRD Tata Innovation Award, CSIR Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Award, and numerous national and international accolades.

Has been ranked among the 100 top global innovation leaders. Author of over 200 research papers and holder of several patents in polymer engineering.

Director General, Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) — 1995 to 2006. During his tenure, CSIR became one of the most innovative R&D organisations globally.

Member, Scientific Advisory Council to the Prime Minister of India. Board member of several multinational corporations and global research institutions.

President, Indian National Science Academy. Chairman, National Innovation Foundation. Member of the Global Council, World Economic Forum.

Associated with leading international bodies including the UN Advisory Panel on Science & Technology, and advisory boards of MIT, Cambridge, and IITs across India.
